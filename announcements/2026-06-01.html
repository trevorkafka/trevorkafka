Cancellation Policy

Situation Lesson Fee
Cancel at least 24 hours in advance No charge
Cancel with less than 24 hours notice Full ordinary class rate (even for free classes)

No-shows and late cancellations do not use up your free classes.

Want more clarity? Here's an example.

In the late cancellation and no-show cases, you are charged the full rate for missing the lesson, but you still have a free class waiting for your next attended lesson.

Important Details

  • Free class discounts are applied manually after each attended lesson — they won’t appear on sign-up.
  • Each student’s discount is individual and cannot be combined or shared.
  • Maximum one free class per calendar day.
  • Unused free classes expire at the end of June 2026 and do not roll over.

Additional 10% off Summer 2026 lessons

Pay for 9 lessons upfront at the standard rate and get charged a 90% rate for lessons until August 31, 2026.

  • Your prepaid balance is applied to lessons at 90% of the regular rate through August 31, 2026.
  • After August 31, any remaining prepaid balance carries over and is applied to lessons at the regular 100% rate.
  • Please note that tutoring sessions will not be available July 15 – August 20, 2026.
  • This discount can be combined with the June free lesson discount described above.
  • Please email to set up prepayment.
