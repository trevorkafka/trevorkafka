More About Introductory Calls

Your first step is to book a free 15-minute introductory call with me where we will discuss your current learning goals, coursework to date (if applicable), the classes that I teach, how I can best help you, teaching methods, scheduling, billing, and anything else you'd like to discuss.

These sessions are free of charge and are non-binding. Please note that this 15-minute intro call is not a tutoring session, so no formal instruction will be provided. Whether or not you choose to pursue tutoring after our intro call is 100% up to you. This time is intended to be a way to

  • set goals,
  • share background information,
  • get the chance to ask/answer any questions we may have, and
  • learn how we can form the perfect team.
I'm looking forward to meeting you and helping YOU make a breakthrough in your own success!

Please feel free to email me at if you would like to chat via email beforehand for any reason. If you are in urgent need of tutoring and would like to skip the intro call process, please email me and I can provide futher instructions.

Introductory calls are held through Zoom. Please make sure to have Zoom installed on either your computer or mobile device in order to attend our meeting. Future tutoring sessions will also be held through Zoom.
