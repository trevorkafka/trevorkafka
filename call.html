Why do I need to enter billing details in order to submit this form?

Please note that inputting billing details is required in order to submit a registration form, but no fees are charged to you unless you decide to continue with formal tutoring sessions. Here are some more details.

  • Sign-up and introductory calls are free and non-binding and you will NOT be billed unless you take future tutoring sesions.
  • Having credit card information already filled out makes it easier in the future for families to make payments once payments are due after tutoring sessions have been taken (this ultimately helps avoid situations where I have to track down clients for payment information in the case a bill ends up being overdue for an extended period of time).
  • Payments will NOT be automatically charged to your card unless you enroll in automatic payments in your profile settings after submitting your registration form.
  • You are in control of when payments are made.
