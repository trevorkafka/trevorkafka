How do I enroll in automatic payments?

After logging in,

  1. click your name at the top-right of the screen,
  2. click My Preferences,
  3. click Manage Payment Methods, and finally
  4. select Enable Auto Pay in the dropdown for the card that you would like to make automatic payments with.

How do I make a one-time payment?

The easiest way to make payments is by using the link provided in the invoice PDF sent to your email, which even allows payment without needing to log in. If you do find yourself logged in and wanting to make a payment, please consult the following steps.

How can I synchronize my class calendar with my calendar, such as Google Calendar or iCal?

  1. Navigate to Calendar from the main menu on the left.
  2. Click Sync Calendar in the toolbar.
  3. Select your device/calendar from the drop-down menu.
Please note that due to limitations set by Google, you will only see updates to your synced calendar once every 24 hours on Google Calendar.
