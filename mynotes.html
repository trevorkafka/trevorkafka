My Class Notes

This page is for current and past tutoring students. Comprehensive notes and class materials from our tutoring sessions are online for access at any time. To find the link to your folder, type your first and last name below.

How are my notes organized?

For most students, Google Drive folders will be organized by month. If we are working on a full course together from scratch, the content may be organized by unit instead.

  • Handwritten notes taken by me during class titled ⭐ Class Notes.pdf are organized in chronological order. All handwritten notes from the entire month are in this single file.
  • Zoom screenshots (especially from when I do on-screen annotations when you share your screen) are organized into folders titled YYYY-MM-DD screenshots. All screenshots from a single day are in a single subfolder.
  • Additional files that vary on a student-to-student basis may also be included.
  • A subfolder titled teacher notes is sometimes present — these are files I use for my own preparation and may be ignored.
What if the form doesn't work?

The form may not work if you haven't taken a class with me since June 2025. Please email and I will assist as soon as possible.
