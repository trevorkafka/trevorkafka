My Class Notes

Tutoring students can access their class notes and materials at any time by filling out the form below.

Comprehensive notes and class materials from our tutoring sessions (both present and past) are stored on Google Drive and are available for access by students at any time. A Google Drive account is not needed to view our files on Google Drive. To find the link to your Google Drive folder, type your first and last name below.

Please note: Since this functionality on my website is new, this form may not work if you haven't taken a class with me since June 2025.

For most students, Google Drive folders will be organized by month. Your folder may contain some of the following items.

  • Handwritten notes taken by me during class titled ‚≠ê Class Notes.pdf. These notes are organized in chronological order. All handwritten notes from the entire month are in this single file.
  • Zoom screenshots (especially from when I do on-screen annotations). These are organized into folders titled YYYY.MM.DD screenshots. All screenshots from a single day are organized into a single subfolder.
  • Additional files that vary on a student-to-student basis.
  • A subfolder titled teacher notes, which can be ignored.
