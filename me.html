What I do for work

I'm a self-employed and work-from-home online physics and math tutor. I spend a good fraction of the year working remotely from various locations around the world and the remainder of the time from my home base Somerville, MA, USA.

More information

This is a career path that branched off of my two years as a high school physics teacher at Wellesley High School in 2014–2016.

Most of my teaching time is focused on Sundays 9:00am–4:00pm and Mondays–Thursdays 3:00pm-10:00pm, late August through early June. It's unfortunately not the best work schedule from the perspective of being able to socalize, but I'm passionate about this job enough that it is worth it for me.

You can learn more about my work on my main website at www.trevorkafka.com.

How did I become a tutor?

I tutored part time consistently from 2015 through 2020 while progressively transitioning from mostly physics students to mostly math students and was tutoring students either in their own homes or in public spaces (libraries, coffee shops, etc). It was a LOT of driving!

Once the pandemic hit in 2020, I pivoted to full time tutoring and also took the business online, which is what I continue to do today.
