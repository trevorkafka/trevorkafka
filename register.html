New Student Registration

  • As of December 2021 it is now required to input billing details in order to submit a registration form.
  • Sign-up is free and your card will NOT be charged in order to sign-up. This only serves to make it easier in the future for families to make payments once payments are due.
  • Payments will NOT be automatically charged to your card unless you enroll in automatic payments in your profile settings after submitting your registration form. You are in control of when payments are made.
  • Please do not fill out the registration form unless you have reached out to me first via email at or through an intro call to ensure an appropriate fit.
